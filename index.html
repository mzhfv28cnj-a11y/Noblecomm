WiFi that reaches the shop

In rural Iowa, the work happens in the shop, the barn, and the outbuilding — and that's exactly where home WiFi gives up. We install professional point-to-point wireless bridges that carry your fiber connection across the yard: weather-rated hardware, clean mounting, real throughput where you actually need it.

Why it matters

The last hundred feet are the hardest

A fiber network is judged by its final connection. Drop placement and premises work happen on private property — inside someone's home or business — where craftsmanship, care, and communication matter as much as splice loss. That's the part of FTTH we specialize in: every install tested, documented, and left clean, so the network owner's reputation is protected at every address.
